Mechanics of Existence

"Mechanics of Existence" — Academic writing project. Mechanics of Existence is a long-term independent writing and research project focused on systems-level thinking across physics, neuroscience, and philosophy. The project explores how complex phenomena can be understood through shared structural principles, with an emphasis on clarity, internal consistency, and first-principles reasoning rather than speculation. The work is conducted part-time alongside full-time employment and formal studies, and is intentionally structured as a slow, long-horizon project. Current efforts are centered on literature review, conceptual mapping, and outlining core questions, with the long-term goal of producing a coherent manuscript or paper once the framework is sufficiently mature. This project reflects a sustained interest in analytical problem-solving, structured thinking, and the ability to work independently on complex ideas over extended periods of time.
